# Debt Payoff Planner

Debt Payoff Planner provides educational estimates and general financial planning support only.

## Not Financial, Legal, or Tax Advice
The app does not provide professional financial, legal, tax, or investment advice.

## Estimates and Assumptions
All calculations are based on user-entered values and assumptions. Results may differ from real-world outcomes due to fees, compounding methods, promotional rates, penalties, payment timing, or creditor policy changes.

## User Responsibility
Before making financial decisions, verify details with your card issuer, mortgage servicer, or a licensed professional.

## No Guarantees
Debt Payoff Planner does not guarantee savings, payoff timelines, or financial outcomes.
